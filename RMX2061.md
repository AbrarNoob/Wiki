### Installation Guide For Project Elixir on Realme 6 Pro

1. Reboot/Boot to Recovery
2. Clear Dalvik, Cache, Data & Internal Storage
3. Then Format Data (Type Yes to Confirm)
4. Once Again reboot to recovery
5. Copy The ZIP
6. Flash
7. Once again Clear Dalvik, Cache, Data & Internal Storag
8. Reboot to System

### Note: 
- Current Recovery (OFRP R11.1_1 Beta) Doesn't Support Decryption For Android 13 Roms.
- If you are coming from A12 to A13 clean flash is compulsory and format data.
- If you are encrypted do format Data before flashing build to avoid bugs, still u can dirty flash over encrypted but mostly people mess it everytime so i skipped that.
