### Installation Guide For Project Elixir on Davinci

### Clean Flash
1. update to latest Steve TWRP.
2. wipe dalvik, cache, system, vendor, data.
3. flash ROM.
4. format data.
5. flash dfe or Magisk (optional).

### Dirty Flash
1. update to latest Steve TWRP.
2. wipe dalvik, cache, system, vendor, data.
3. flash ROM.
4. flash dfe or Magisk (optional).
