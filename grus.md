### Installation Guide For Project-Elixir on MI 9 SE (Grus)

## Clean flash (New flash)
1. Reboot to Recovery (Use TWRP)
2. Flash FW (12.5.4 cn recommended)
3. Flash Rom
4. Format data
5. Reboot and enjoy :)

## Dirty flash (Update)
1. Reboot to Recovery (Use TWRP)
2. Flash Rom
3. Reboot and enjoy :)
