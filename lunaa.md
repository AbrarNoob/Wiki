### Installation Guide For Project Elixir on lunaa(RMX3360-RMX3363)

### RECOMMENDED CLEAN FLASH FROM A12 TO A13

### Clean Flash
1. Flash lasted LOS Recovery(lunaa based)
2. Reboot to Recovery
3. Format data
4. Sideload ROM.zip 
5. Reboot System

### Dirty Flash
1. Reboot to recovery
2. Sideload ROM.zip 
3. Reboot System
