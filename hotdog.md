### Installation Guide For Project Elixir on OnePlus 7T Pro (hotdog)

```
- Being on OOS12 F22
- bootloader unlock
- fastboot flash recovery_a recovery.img
- fastboot flash recovery_b recovery.img
- reboot recovery
- adb sideload rom.zip
- factory reset
- reboot system
```
